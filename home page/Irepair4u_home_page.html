About

We are your local smartphone repair shop, serving local communities of Lakewood, Jackson, Howell and Tom's River for over seven years with a professionalism and honesty. We repair anything from broken screens to faulty batteries, charging ports, water damage and much more. We are located in a beautiful and quiet street just few minutes away from Lakewood township.
